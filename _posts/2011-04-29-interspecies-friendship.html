Interspecies friendships are some of the most fantastic things ever. Firstly, they're animals, which in and of themselves are amazing. However, having different species interact and seeing them get along is even more amazing. It's not simply coexistence, even though that is not quite as trite for humans as it sounds, but genuine friendships woven together without the social context – or even oral communication – to which humans are so accustomed.

This MSNBC article has a short blurb and pictures of about twenty different cases of interspecies relationships. The Telegraph has a few to share as well.

I hope I see some of this during the summer, as I plan to frequent the park near uptown when the weather is warmer, and because I seek vengeance for the slobber that was left on my hand (but by the miniature ponies – the donkey was innocent).
